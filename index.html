A Legacy Reimagined

KokoPass was not just an idea. It was deeply Rooted in history of the pacific culture, our founder grew up under the shade of his grandfather's cacao trees, learning that every pod carried the DNA of a thousand years of Samoan agricultural tradition.

But tradition alone wasn't enough to secure the future for local farmers. In 2026, KokoPass won the BYU Hawaii 'Empower Your Dreams' competition with a simple but radical idea: give every farmer a digital passport for their harvest.

Today, we are working to bridge the gap between ancestral knowledge and global markets, ensuring that every batch of Koko is verified, valued, and completely transparent.

"We aren't just selling cacao; we are sharing the soul of the Pacific with the world."
